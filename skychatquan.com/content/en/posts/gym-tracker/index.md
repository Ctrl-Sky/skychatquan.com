The Purpose for Creation

I really enjoy working out, and I thought about getting an app to keep track of my workouts. However, I found that many of the apps are very cluttered with extra features I don’t really need and missing features I would want to see. So, I created my own app for keeping track of my workouts. This app allows me to put focus onto features I enjoy more, such as stats on my weight progression and even a calendar tracking the workouts I’ve been on.
